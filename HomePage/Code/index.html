Some of Indonesian heritage that go International

Tempe ---

Tempe is a food made from soybeans can be combined in a variety of ways. A lot of indonesian people used to deep fried this dish. But did you know, Japanese people make this dish one of their favorites. Starting from someone who sold "tempe" in a Japanese culinary bazaar. Once tried, they liked it immediately.

Saman ---

This traditional dance originating from Aceh has been designated as a cultural heritage of Indonesia by UNESCO since 2011. This dance reflects education, religion, manners, heroism, cohesiveness and togetherness.Saman dance is one of the dances that is quite unique, because it only displays clapping other movements

Lorentz National Park ---

Lorentz National Park is located in the province of Papua, Indonesia. With an area of ​​2.4 million hectares. Lorentz is the largest national park in Southeast Asia. The park is still uncharted, explored and contains many native plants, animals and cultures. Lorentz National Park is representative of the most comprehensive ecosystem for biodiversity in Asia Pacific. This area is also one of three regions in the world that has glaciers in the tropics.
