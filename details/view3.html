Indonesian Foods

Belows are typical food from Indonesia

Sate is a food made of meat that is cut into small pieces and skewered in such a way with a stick of coconut or bamboo and then grilled using wood charcoal coals.

Rendang is a meat dish originating from Minangkabau. This dish is made with long low-temperature cooking process using various spices, meats and coconut milk.

Fried rice is a food in the form of rice that is fried and stirred in cooking oil, margarine or butter, usually added with sweet soy sauce, onion, salt, eggs, chicken, and crackers.

Pempek or empek-empek is a typical Palembang food made from finely ground fish meat and starch, as well as several other ingredients such as eggs, mashed garlic, flavorings and salt.

Gado-gado is a typical food originating from Indonesia in the form of vegetables that are boiled and mixed together, with peanut sauce or sauce from peanuts and which are mashed.

Bika Ambon is a typical dish from Medan, Indonesia. Bika Ambon is generally made with a pandan flavor, although now there are other flavors such as durian, cheese, and chocolate.
